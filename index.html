Host Instructions

1 Share the Player Link: Copy and share the player link above with all participants.
2 Start a New Game: Click "New Game" to reset the game and prepare for players.
3 Call Numbers Manually: Enter a number (1-90) and click "Call Number" or click on numbers in the grid.
4 Monitor Players: Watch the player list to see who's joined and how many tickets they have.
5 Announce Winners: The system will automatically detect and announce winners.
